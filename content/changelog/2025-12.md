## Bulk Records Import

![Import Records in Bulk](/images/changelog/2025-12/import-records.jpg)

![Records History](/images/changelog/2025-12/records-history.jpg)

Import is no longer limited to positions. You can now bulk import your records.

- Import multiple records at once. This makes it much faster to **backfill your net worth history** or migrate from spreadsheets.
- **Improved feedback during import**, making it clearer what was added and what failed, instead of silently skipping records.

Kudos to [@gionn](https://github.com/gionn) for his help in releasing this feature and supporting Foliofox's Developer Experience.
