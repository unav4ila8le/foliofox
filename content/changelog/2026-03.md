## Scenario Planning Improvements

![Scenario Planning with Growth and Inflation Assumptions](/images/changelog/2026-03/scenario-planning-assumptions.jpg)

More control over how you model the future.

- You can now include growth and inflation assumptions in your scenario planning.
- This makes projections more realistic and adaptable to different market conditions.

## Export Records

![Export Records](/images/changelog/2026-03/export-records.jpg)

Take your data with you.

- You can now export your portfolio records in bulk.
- Useful for backups, external analysis, or migrations.

## Net Worth Performance View

![Net Worth Performance View](/images/changelog/2026-03/portfolio-performance.jpg)

Your net worth, now with real performance insights.

- The net worth chart can now be toggled to show performance instead of absolute value.
- Foliofox calculates time-weighted returns for your market-backed assets (with ticker symbols).
- You can analyze performance across different timeframes, not just raw net worth changes.

This is a foundational step toward more advanced portfolio analytics and performance benchmarks.
