## Daily Net Worth Tracking

![Daily Net Worth Tracking](/images/changelog/2025-11/daily-net-worth.jpg)

A complete upgrade of how Foliofox tracks your net worth.

- Your chart now uses **daily data points** instead of weekly snapshots.
- This gives you way **more accurate history**, especially if your assets change often.
- The chart loads **much faster** thanks to a backend + caching optimization.

## Shareable Portfolio Links (Public Sharing)

![Shareable Portfolio Links (Public Sharing)](/images/changelog/2025-11/sharable-portfolio-links.jpg)

![Public Portfolio](/images/changelog/2025-11/public-portfolio.jpg)

Stop taking screenshots or photos of your screen (iykyk). Share your portfolio the clean way.

- You can now **enable public sharing** directly from your dashboard.
- Foliofox generates a **unique link** with an expiration date, perfect for Reddit posts, forums, or showing a friend.
- You can **edit the expiration, disable the link, or even customize your link name** (first come, first served).
- No personal info is included.
