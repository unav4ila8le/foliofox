## Broker Import

![Broker Import](/images/changelog/2026-06/broker-import.jpg)

A much easier way to keep Foliofox in sync.

- We added the first version of Broker Import.
- You can now import your portfolio and buy/sell transaction history from supported broker exports.
- Foliofox can create missing positions, import transaction records, skip duplicates, and recalculate your portfolio history automatically.
- The first supported broker is Trade Republic.

This means you can export your data from your broker, import it into Foliofox, and repeat the process whenever you want to keep your portfolio updated.

Trade Republic support was made possible thanks to a Foliofox community member who shared their export file and helped me build the first broker adapter.

If your broker is not supported yet, please feel free to [join our Discord](/discord) or send me an email at [leonardo@foliofox.com](mailto:leonardo@foliofox.com).

## Custom Categories

![Custom Categories](/images/changelog/2026-06/custom-categories.jpg)

Organize your portfolio your way.

- You can now create your own custom categories.
- Assets can be labeled using the categories that make sense to you.
- This gives you more flexibility than the default Foliofox category system.

## 🇬🇧 London and 🇰🇼 Kuwait Market Support

- Foliofox now supports London-listed securities.
- GBp / GBX-denominated securities are now supported.
- Foliofox now supports Kuwait-listed securities, including securities quoted in KWF / Kuwaiti fils.
