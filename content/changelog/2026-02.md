## Projected Income Stacked Bar Chart

![Projected Income Stacked Bar Chart](/images/changelog/2026-02/projected-income-stacked-bar-chart.jpg)

Clarity on where your dividend income actually comes from.

- You can now view projected dividend income as a **stacked bar chart**.
- Each month clearly shows which positions contribute to your total income.
- This makes it easier to spot **concentration**, **seasonality**, and **dependency** on specific assets.

If dividend income is part of your strategy, this gives you a much clearer picture.

## Net Worth Before and After Capital Gains Taxes

![Net Worth Before and After Capital Gains Taxes](/images/changelog/2026-02/net-worth-after-tax.jpg)

A more realistic view of your wealth.

- You can now add an **optional capital gains tax rate** to any position.
- With a simple toggle, you can **switch between net worth before taxes and net worth after taxes**.
- This helps you understand what your portfolio would actually be worth if all positions were liquidated.

Especially useful if you actively rebalance or plan partial exits.

## AI Advisor Improvements

![Use AI to Import Spredsheet Positions](/images/changelog/2026-02/ai-spreadsheet-import.jpg)

![Attach Files (Images and PDFs) to your AI Advisor Chat](/images/changelog/2026-02/ai-attachements.jpg)

Smarter, more capable, and more flexible.

- **Upgraded to a new AI model with improved reasoning and structured analysis.**
- The AI position import now supports spreadsheets (CSV, TSV, XLSX, Excel formats), in addition to images and PDFs.
- The AI Advisor chat now **supports file uploads** directly inside the conversation (images and PDFs for now).

This moves the Advisor closer to becoming a true portfolio copilot.

## 🇺🇳 Localized Number Inputs

Small detail, big usability improvement.

- Number inputs now **automatically adapt to your regional format**.
- Proper grouping and decimal separators are applied as you type.

Manual data entry is now smoother and safer.
